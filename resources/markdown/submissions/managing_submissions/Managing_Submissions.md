# Somehow I Manage... Submissions

View, organize, and manage form submissions efficiently with Flow Forms' powerful submission management tools.

All form submissions you have permission to access are available from the **Submissions** page. Click **{{icon:paper-clip size-4 inline-block align-text-center}} Submissions** from the sidebar to begin.

***Note:** You can also click **clear filters** when accessing the Submissions page from the sidebar. Flow Forms automatically saves and displays your last search and filter results for easy access to frequently needed information. If you're starting a new search, clicking **clear filters** from the sidebar saves time and prevents you from having to select **{{icon:x-circle size-4 inline-block align-text-center}} Clear Filters** after the submissions page loads.*


## Control Panel
At the top of your submissions page is the **Control Panel**, which remains visible as you scroll through your submissions. This area contains the [search bar](Submissions/Search_Bar), all the [filter](Submissions/Filters) functions, [export and print](Submissions/Export_Print) options, and controls to change the [display](Submissions/Display_Options) view and sort order. 

The bottom of the control panel includes the [Bulk Actions Toolbar](Submissions/Bulk-Actions-Toolbar) which allows you to take actions on multiple submissions rather than dealing with them individually. *This section only remains visible while scrolling if at least one submission is selected.*

Below the control panel is an abbreviated display of all form submissions you're authorized to access (20 entries per page). By default, submissions are listed in order of most recent activity, but this can be filtered and sorted in various ways.
