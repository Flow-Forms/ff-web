## Export

The **{{icon:chart-bar size-4 inline-block align-text-center}} Export** button, located above the right end of the Submissions Control Panel, allows you to download form submissions as a CSV file. The export data will include all form fields, timestamps, submission metadata and status information.

To initiate an export, you must first filter your submissions [by form.](submissions/managing_submissions/filters#forms) If you want to further narrow your dataset, select additional criteria from **Filters**. Once the desired data is displayed, click **Export**.

If successful, this message will appear: 
>Export started successfully. This may take a few minutes. The result will be emailed upon completion. Check your notification center if you do not see the email.

Your export will not work without a form filter selected. If no form is selected, this message will appear:
>Please select at least one form to export

When successful, you'll receive an email from Flow Forms at your account's email address containing a link to download your CSV file. File size will affect delivery time to your inbox.

Exports are also available in your **Notification Center**, accessible by clicking your user name in the upper left corner. Exports ready for download are listed under **Submission Exports** and can be downloaded from this location.


## Print 
The **{{icon:printer size-4 inline-block align-text-center}} Print** button (beside **Export** at the top right of the Submissions Control Panel) allows you to download and print individual submissions or groups of submissions as a PDF.

**To print filtered submissions:** Use the filtering options to display your desired results, then click **Print** to print them all at once. A modal window will display the number of submissions in your print request with options to **Cancel** (exit without printing) or **Start Print Job** (proceed with printing). A download progress bar will show your request's progress, and when complete, a **Download PDF** button will appear. The modal also provides access to _Recent Print Jobs_ for re-downloading.

**To print selected submissions:** Select desired submissions by clicking their individual checkboxes (to the left of the form title in each submission's header). When your selections are made (checkmarks will appear), click **Print** and follow the process above.

**To exclude a few submissions:** Use **Select All** by checking the checkbox in the **Bulk Actions Toolbar** above the submissions, then uncheck those you wish to exclude before printing.

**To print a single submission:** Click the gear icon {{icon:cog-6-tooth size-4 inline-block align-text-center}} on the right side of that submission's header and select **Print** or **Download as PDF** from the menu.
